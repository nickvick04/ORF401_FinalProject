The Problem

Checkout is broken.
Everyone knows it.

😤

Self-Checkout Is Widely Hated

Customers are forced to do the work themselves — often without knowing how — on systems they find confusing and impersonal. The frustration is real and measurable. People actively avoid stores because of it.

⏳

Wait Times Drive People Away

Long lines push customers toward online shopping, delivery apps, and eating out. Every minute spent waiting at checkout is a reason not to come back — and a reason to tell others not to, either.

💸

Cashiers Are Expensive and Slow

Human cashiers are the single largest labor cost in retail. They create bottlenecks at peak hours and set a hard ceiling on how many customers a store can serve simultaneously.

🚫

Existing Alternatives Fall Short

RFID tags are expensive and impractical at grocery scale. Amazon Go requires massive proprietary infrastructure most stores can't afford. Existing systems treat checkout as an afterthought — we built a company around solving it.

Our Products

Three products.
One seamless system.

Most stores begin with our app-based system and expand into computer vision and automation over time — each product is designed to build on the last. Stores can adopt any combination — start with one product, add more as you grow. Each module is independent, integrates with your existing setup, and pairs with our hardware partners for best-in-class performance.

Product 01 📷

Computer Vision Checkout

AI-powered camera software that tracks every item placed in a cart in real time — at the shelves, in the aisles, and at the exit. Members can be recognized on entry and supported throughout their shopping session. When paired with our app and camera partners, stores can progressively reduce manual scanning and move toward low-interaction checkout.

Works with most modern cameras. We recommend our hardware partners for best accuracy. Stores can also choose camera-only entry/exit coverage without full aisle deployment. We partner with select camera manufacturers to offer pre-integrated systems, while still supporting integration with existing in-store hardware.

In-Aisle CV Entry / Exit AI Auto Charge
Product 02 📱

Membership & App System

The customer-facing membership platform. Shoppers register, link a payment method, and use the app to scan items as they shop. The CV system ties their identity to their session — multiple profiles per account are supported and recognized automatically.This is the foundation of the ZeroQueue system — enabling app-based item scanning today while preparing stores for deeper automation over time.

Optional hardware add-on: entrance kiosk terminals for customers without smartphones, available through our hardware partners.

Mobile App Multi-Profile Card on File
Product 03 🛡️

Risk & Theft Mitigation AI

Intelligent alert dampening that protects loyal customers from false accusations — which can destroy a business far faster than any theft. The AI cross-references membership history before escalating anything to staff.

Unregistered exits receive full scrutiny. Known members are protected. Pursue real theft confidently, without risking the customer relationships that are your biggest asset.
